Account Questions

How do I change my email address?

To change your email address:

  1. Go to your profile by clicking on your name in the top right
  2. Navigate to the "Email & Password" tab
  3. Enter your new email address and current password
  4. Click "Update Email"

You'll receive a verification email to confirm your new address.

How do I reset my password?

There are two ways to reset your password:

If you're logged in:

  1. Go to your profile
  2. Select the "Email & Password" tab
  3. Enter your current password and new password
  4. Click "Update Password"

If you're not logged in:

  1. Click "Log In" at the top of the page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Go to your profile
  2. Select the "Account" tab
  3. Scroll down to the "Delete Account" section
  4. Click "Delete Account" and confirm your decision

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any active bookings must be completed or canceled before deletion.

Booking Questions

How do I book a car?

Booking a car is simple:

  1. From the dashboard, enter your location or use your current location
  2. Select your preferred pickup date and time
  3. Choose your booking duration
  4. Click "Find Cars" to see available vehicles
  5. Select a car from the list or map view
  6. Review the booking details and price
  7. Confirm your booking by clicking "Book Now"

You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your booking.

How do I cancel a booking?

To cancel a booking:

  1. Go to "My Bookings" in your dashboard
  2. Find the booking you want to cancel
  3. Click "View Details"
  4. Click the "Cancel Booking" button
  5. Confirm your cancellation

Note: Our cancellation policy provides:

  • Full refund if canceled 24+ hours before pickup
  • 50% refund if canceled between 3-24 hours before pickup
  • No refund if canceled less than 3 hours before pickup

  • Debit Cards
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay (on iOS devices)
  • Google Pay (on Android devices)

All payments are securely processed and encrypted.

How does the pricing work?

Our pricing is transparent and based on several factors:

  • Base Rate: Each vehicle has an hourly rate
  • Duration: Longer bookings may have discounted hourly rates
  • Insurance: Basic insurance is included in the price
  • Location: Some high-demand areas may have higher rates
  • Time: Peak times may have higher rates than off-peak

The final price is always shown before you confirm your booking, with no hidden fees.
